About OSDConf

OSDC organised OSSCamp Delhi in 2010, with other open-source communities around. The unconference was loaded with FOSS spirit, open hacks and free swag.

OSDConf, as a fresh edition of attempts by OSDC to promote FOSS, is coming to revive the same energy.
